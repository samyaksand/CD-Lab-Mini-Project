OBJECTIVES

The main objectives of this experiment are:

  • To fully understand the logic and design of Shift Reduce Parsers.
  • To use the simulator to analyse the grammer entered by the user
  • To gain knowledge about Bottom-up parsing.
  • To understand the functioning of a Shift Reduce Parser with the help of a simulator.
